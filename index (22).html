🏛️ Our Legacy of Excellence

Welcome to Adichunchanagiri College PU College (ACPUC), Arsikere - where academic excellence meets holistic development. Our institution is dedicated to providing world-class education and comprehensive support for students pursuing their dreams in science and competitive examinations.

🏢 World-Class Facilities

🔬 Advanced Research Laboratories

Cutting-edge physics, chemistry, and biology laboratories equipped with the latest instruments, safety protocols, and research facilities for comprehensive hands-on learning and scientific exploration.

📚 Digital Learning Library

Extensive library featuring thousands of books, international journals, digital databases, e-learning resources, and quiet study spaces designed to support academic research and intellectual growth.

💻 High-Tech Computer Centers

Modern computer laboratories with high-performance systems, ultra-fast internet connectivity, latest software applications, and dedicated spaces for programming and digital learning activities.

🏃‍♂️ Comprehensive Sports Complex

Full-scale sports facilities including basketball courts, volleyball courts, badminton halls, cricket grounds, athletics track, gymnasium, and outdoor recreational areas promoting physical fitness and team spirit.

🍽️ Premium Dining Facilities

Hygienic, spacious cafeteria serving nutritious and delicious meals with diverse menu options, special dietary accommodations, and professional food service maintaining the highest quality standards.

🚌 Safe Transportation Network

Comprehensive transportation service covering extensive routes throughout Arsikere and surrounding areas, featuring modern, well-maintained buses with GPS tracking and experienced drivers ensuring student safety.

🏥 Complete Medical Support

On-campus medical facility staffed by qualified healthcare professionals, equipped with modern medical equipment, and maintaining partnerships with leading hospitals for comprehensive healthcare coverage.

🎭 Cultural Activity Centers

Dedicated spaces for cultural activities including auditoriums, music rooms, dance studios, art workshops, and exhibition halls fostering creativity and artistic expression among students.

🔒 Advanced Security Systems

Comprehensive security infrastructure featuring 24/7 surveillance, controlled access systems, trained security personnel, emergency response protocols, and safety measures ensuring complete campus security.
